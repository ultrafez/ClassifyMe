Classification Calculator for University of Sheffield degrees

Calculate your final University of Sheffield degree classification by entering your module results.

This tool can only be used for standard 3-year undergraduate degrees, and 4-year integrated Master's degrees (e.g. MEng, MComp) that don't include a year in industry or year abroad.

Everything you enter will automatically be saved, so next time you visit this page your modules will still be here.

Your modules won't be saved if you browse away from this page, but if you visit this page in a more modern browser such as Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox, then your changes will be saved automatically.
